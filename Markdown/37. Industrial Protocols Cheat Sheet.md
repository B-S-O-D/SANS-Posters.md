This tri-fold cheat sheet provides details about
common Industrial protocols that are be found in
different areas of control networks. These protocols
have been organized in the following sections:

•
Fieldbus and Fieldbus Management
(Purdue Levels 0-1)
•
Site SCADA (Purdue Levels 2-3)
•
Regional SCADA (Purdue Levels 2-3)
Details vary by protocol but include names, ethernet
types, port numbers, and available encryption
capabilities. For additional details about each protocol
refer to the associated protocol specifications, vendor
specifications, and other online documentation.
NOTE: Data provided in these tables have been taken
from publicly available information to be used as a
quick reference. Blank fields may mean that the
information is not applicable or not available. Please
contact the authors with any additional details about
these and other protocols. Please provide supporting
references. Applicable fields and corrections will be
updated to future versions of this cheat sheet.
